About This Project

Context & Objectives

This work was conducted during a four-month internship at LPRL Serang (Loka Penelitian Sumber Daya dan Kerentanan Pesisir), focusing on spatial documentation of coastal ecosystems in Jawa Tengah province.

The primary objectives were to compile, validate, and visualize distribution data for key coastal habitats — mangrove, seagrass, and coral reef — and to develop a remote sensing tool for detecting shoreline dynamics outside established spatial planning boundaries.

The accretion analysis specifically examines land changes occurring outside the RTRWP (Rencana Tata Ruang Wilayah Provinsi) boundary, providing insight into unplanned coastal expansion and potential policy implications.
